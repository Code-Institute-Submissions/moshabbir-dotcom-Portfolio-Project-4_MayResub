About Our History

JA Therapies was formed from a dream to offer holistic treaments that are rooted in recognised healthcare principles. All our staff are externally trained and validated by accredited training providers.

Our small and friendly team of professionals each have their own specialisms and accreditations within their respective fields in specialist treatment industries, so rest assured you are in the right hands when choosing us for your therapeutic needs.

We don't believe in treatment for treatments sake... We believe in specialising your treatments to design a bespoke program to maximise the benefits to your health & wellbeing.

All products used are formulated in house from organic ingredients to suit your specific requirments.

  • Fully trained staff
  • Fully Insured
  • Range of specialisms
  • Male and Female therapists
  • Products formulated to individual requirements
